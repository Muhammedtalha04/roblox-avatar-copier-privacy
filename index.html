Privacy Policy for Roblox Avatar Copier

Last Updated: May 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Roblox Avatar Copier ("we", "our", or "extension") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Our extension collects the following types of information:

  • Authentication Information: We access Roblox authentication cookies to make authenticated API requests to Roblox's servers. This is necessary to verify item ownership and apply avatar changes to your account.
  • Web History: We detect when you are on Roblox avatar pages to enable the extension's functionality.
  • User Activity: We monitor your interactions with the Roblox site related to avatar selections and customizations.
  • Website Content: We read and process content from Roblox pages, including avatar information and item lists.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Enabling you to copy avatars from other Roblox users
  • Checking which items you already own
  • Showing you which items you need to purchase
  • Applying avatar changes to your Roblox account
  • Saving your preferences for the extension

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not transmit your data to our servers. The only external communications are with official Roblox API endpoints.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your information with any third parties. All communication is strictly between the extension and official Roblox servers.

User Controls

You can control your data in the following ways:

  • Clear the extension's cache through the settings panel
  • Disable or remove the extension at any time through Chrome's extension management
  • Adjust which permissions the extension has access to in Chrome's extension settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
