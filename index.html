🔒 Privacy Policy

Roblox Avatar Copier Browser Extension

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Roblox Avatar Copier! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our browser extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Important: Roblox Avatar Copier is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Roblox Corporation. We are an independent third-party extension designed to enhance your Roblox experience.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally

Our extension stores the following information locally in your browser:

  • User Preferences: Language settings, theme preferences (including premium themes), dark mode settings
  • Avatar Cache: Temporarily cached avatar data for improved performance
  • Usage Settings: Last used profile ID, advanced settings configurations
  • Authentication Data: Roblox authentication cookies (only when you're logged into Roblox)
  • Theme Data: Information about purchased premium themes and theme preferences

2.2 Data We Access

To provide our services, we access:

  • Roblox Profile Information: Public avatar data, item information, and outfit details
  • Inventory Data: Information about items you own (to check missing items)
  • Clipboard Data: Only when you choose to paste profile URLs or user IDs
  • Active Tab Information: Only when you're on Roblox-related pages

2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Roblox account passwords
  • Payment information
  • Browsing history outside of Roblox
  • Personal messages or chat logs

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core Functionality

  • Copy Roblox avatars to your account
  • Preview avatars before copying
  • Check which items you own vs. missing items
  • Apply avatar changes to your Roblox account
  • Manage premium themes and customization options

3.2 Enhanced User Experience

  • Remember your language and theme preferences
  • Cache avatar data for faster loading
  • Provide automatic language detection
  • Enable premium theme features and customizations
  • Store advanced settings for optimal performance

3.3 Performance Optimization

  • Cache frequently accessed data to reduce API calls
  • Optimize loading times for better user experience
  • Manage batch processing for multiple items

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's extension storage API. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We cannot access your personal information
  • Data is encrypted by your browser
  • You have full control over your data

4.2 Security Measures

  • No External Servers: We don't store any of your data on external servers
  • Minimal Permissions: We only request permissions necessary for core functionality
  • Secure API Usage: All Roblox API interactions use official, secure endpoints
  • Cookie Protection: Authentication cookies are handled securely and never transmitted

4.3 Data Retention

Data is retained locally until you:

  • Uninstall the extension
  • Clear your browser data
  • Manually reset extension settings

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Roblox Integration

Our extension interacts with Roblox's official APIs and website to provide its functionality. This interaction is subject to Roblox's own privacy policy and terms of service.

5.2 No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. Your usage patterns and personal data are not collected or shared with any external parties.

6. Your Rights and Controls

6.1 Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all stored data through extension settings
  • Modify: Change preferences and settings at any time
  • Delete: Clear cache and reset settings
  • Export: Data can be backed up through browser sync

6.2 Permission Management

You can manage extension permissions through your browser settings:

  • Disable clipboard access
  • Restrict site access
  • Remove extension entirely

7. Premium Features

Our extension includes premium themes and advanced features. Information about purchased themes is stored locally and is not transmitted to external servers. Premium feature access is managed through secure local verification methods.

8. Children's Privacy

Our extension is designed to be safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we don't collect personal information, our extension provides a safe environment for younger users.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date
  • Significant changes will be communicated through extension updates
  • Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of changes
